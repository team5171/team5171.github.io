🍎 macOS Installation Guide

macOS will block the app initially. Follow these steps to allow it:

1

Try to open Team5171.app. When it says it's blocked, click OK.

2

Open System Settings ⚙️ and go to Privacy & Security 🔒.

3

Scroll to Security. Look for "Team5171.app was blocked..."

4

Click Open Anyway, enter your password 🔑, and click Open again.

⭐ You only need to do this once!
